# 06 — Case Study

This folder contains the final written case study produced at 
the conclusion of the E-Commerce Sales Intelligence project. 
The report documents the full analytical process from raw data 
to business recommendations, written for a business and 
technical audience.

---

## Files in This Folder

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `README.md` | Overview of the case study folder and report structure |
| `case_study_report.md` | Full written case study covering the problem, process, findings, and business recommendations |

---

The case study report is organised into the following sections:

| Section | Title |

## Intended Audience

This case study is written for:

- Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating analytical capability
- SaaS, fintech, and e-commerce teams reviewing portfolio work
- CRM and Revenue Operations professionals assessing 
  business intelligence skills
- Data and operations teams interested in the intersection 
  of customer data and business performance
